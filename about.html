About The Wholesale King Ltd Services

15+ years of excellence in agricultural solutions and logistics

Our Story

Founded in 2009, The Wholesale King Ltd Services began as a small family-owned business with a simple mission: to provide comprehensive agricultural solutions that help farmers and businesses thrive. What started as a greenhouse construction company has evolved into a full-service provider of agricultural infrastructure, fresh produce, and reliable logistics services.

Our founder, John Martinez, recognized the growing need for sustainable agricultural practices and reliable supply chain solutions. With a background in agricultural engineering and a passion for innovation, he built The Wholesale King Ltd Services on the principles of quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.

Today, we serve over 500 clients across the region, from small family farms to large commercial operations, providing them with the tools and services they need to succeed in modern agriculture.
